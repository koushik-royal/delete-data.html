EyeNova Account and Data Deletion

EyeNova allows users to request deletion of their account and associated data at any time.

How to Request Account Deletion

If you wish to delete your EyeNova account and all related information, please send an email request to:

poolavenkatkoushik@gmail.com

Include the following information in your email:

  • Your registered email address used in the application
  • Your request for account deletion

Data That Will Be Deleted

Once your request is processed, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • User profile information
  • Game performance records
  • Exercise progress history
  • Associated account data

Processing Time

Your account and data will be permanently deleted within 7 business days after receiving the request.

If you have questions about data deletion, please contact us at:
